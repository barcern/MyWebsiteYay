    About Us

    A great way to sample wonderful whiskies even on a student budget. We invite professional Brand Ambassadors to host tastings every second week throughout the semester - never with fewer than five drams! Events are friendly and informal gatherings. See our page for details and come along!

    Meetings

    -

    We meet every other Thursday at Hotel du Vin and welcome everyone - whether you are a whisky expert or have never tried it before, feel free to join us and see what we are about!

    Membership is £15. This gets you entry to each of our tastings for only £5 (non-members pay £10), as well as a 7.5% discount at Luvians and a complimentary Glencairn tasting glass worth at least £6.
