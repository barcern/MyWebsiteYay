Next Tasting Tomatin 14.4. at Hotel du Vin
Tomatin is a gem of a distillery, located a little south of Inverness. The name translates as ‘Hill of the Juniper Bush’, but do not think too much of gin – the wood giving off no smoke, the distillery claims these plants were frequently used for illicit distillation before the distillery was founded.

Last Tasting Diageo 31.3. at Hotel du Vin
Known as United Distillers until 1998, Diageo manages a host of big name brands – from Smirnoff to Baileys. It’s the whisky we care about, however, and in this they are probably best known for the 12 distilleries that make up the Classic Malts range.
